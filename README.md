<h3 align="center">Azimuth adds a brand-new planet along with its own solar system and tech tree.
The planet is called Gelion.</h3>
<hr>
<h3 align="center">Overview</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Crush enemy forces and turn the planet into a resource hub for your faction.</li>
  <li>Gradually branching gameplay and campaign progression.</li>
  <li>Discover 12 items, 7 liquids, 12 units, and 85 blocks!</li>
  <li>12 playable sectors to conquer.</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h3 align="center">Features</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Multiblock structures and their variations</li>
  <li>Unit gestators with multiple variants</li>
  <li>Liquid-based energy system (with conversion into standard power)</li>
  <li>Byte system and consumable research mechanics</li>
  <li>Drone stations (shields, healing, buffs)</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h3 align="center">Highlights</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Absolutely BRUTAL visual effects</li>
  <li>Highly polished and visually stunning campaign maps</li>
  <li>Every sprite (blocks & units) is higher quality than vanilla</li>
  <li>Deep unit design: Each unit has unique abilities, mechanics, and a distinct concept</li>
  <li>Complex production chains and conditions: A serious challenge for unprepared players</li>
</ul>
